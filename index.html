About our Institute

Chittagong Polytechnic Institute (CPI), is a government technical institution located in the port city of Chittagong, Bangladesh. It provides theoretical and practical education of basic engineering and technology. Chittagong Polytechnic Institute was established in 1962. It is one of the best public polytechnic institutes in Bangladesh.
